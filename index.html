    Thoughs on Progressive Enhancement

    Progressive enhancement is a great way to envision a model that will help you make content and design that can be as close as universal reach you can get so that people of all flavors can enjoy and digest content created.

    With the M&M analogy is a great analogy based on the fact the content(peanut) is the most important peice of the puzzle. The content is what draws a person to view the page. Some people may like the chocloate M&Ms and with this model of progressive ehancement they could possibly be tempted to try the peanut M&M and be converted instrad of being just plain chocolate.

    To really be good at progressive enhancement you have to have the ability to read the customer beyond what they tell you by other cues. Personality, image and other detail thiungs can give you a map of what to build around when making the M&M. Another ability is to know the time or era that is present, a person definately does not want to be stuck in an era that has passed. In all this was a very helpful article and I enjoyed reading it.
