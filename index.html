ErgoMod Workspaces

About ErgoMod

ErgoMod Workspaces is one of the first to offer an ergonomic modern standing workspace. Let us make your workspace aesthetic and ergonomic with a modern setup. You tell us what you desire to have and we will assemble and deliver to the specified site. We come out and consult with you about your needs and we can develop a plan of action that can fit your needs.We have vendors that we can order standing desks, ergonomic chairs and other workspaces accessories. We can also make custom wooden desktops with standing desk frames upon request along with cable management options as well.

ErgoMod Mission

Our mission is to provide a workspace whether in the office or working home remotely a comfortable workspace that will provide more productivity and less body strain from sitting in a chair for long periods of time. With cable management the workspace can have the most aesthetic look possible.From CEO Gray

ErgoMod Accessories

Accessories for the ErgoMod workspace are endless. We can provide curved monitors to prevent the screen bezel in the middle of dual screens, as curved monitors can be the size of two screens combined. With monitor arms the monitors can be off the desk and increasing workspace. Another set of accessories is Desk lamps, leather desk pads, and footrest and standing pads.

ErgoMod Pricing

Pricing is based on vendors and is subject to change. Once we come onsite and consult with clients we can come with a complete total of total project cost. We will list some of the price of the desks from our vendor. Keep in mind that labor fees are not included but will be once the project is planned and discussed and all fees will be included.
